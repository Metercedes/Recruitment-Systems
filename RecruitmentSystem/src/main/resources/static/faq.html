FAQ

How do I register? Choose "Register as Employer" or "Register as Job Seeker" from the login pages.

How do I filter jobs? On the View Jobs page, use the filters for salary, difficulty, skills, or employer rating.

How do I get notified about new jobs? Update your skills in Settings to receive notifications for matching jobs.

Can I comment on job postings? Yes, on the View Jobs page, add comments to any job listing.

How do I rate an employer? Visit the Ratings page to submit a score and review for any employer.
